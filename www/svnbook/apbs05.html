分支和标签

Subversion不区分文件系统空间和“分支”空间；分支和标签都是普通的文件系统目录，这恐怕是CVS用户需要逾越的最大心理障碍，所有信息在第 4 章 分支与合并。

警告

Since Subversion treats branches and tags as ordinary directories, always remember to check out the trunk (http://svn.example.com/repos/calc/trunk/) of your project, and not the project itself (http://svn.example.com/repos/calc/). If you make the mistake of checking out the project itself, you'll wind up with a working copy that contains a copy of your project for every branch and tag you have.[58]


[58] 如果在检出完成之前没有消耗完磁盘空间的话。
