2.8. Summary

Now we've covered most of the Subversion client commands. Notable exceptions are those dealing with branching and merging (see Chapter 4, Branching and Merging) and properties (see Section 3.2, “Properties”). However, you may want to take a moment to skim through Chapter 9, Subversion Complete Reference to get an idea of all the different commands that Subversion has—and how you can use them to make your work easier.
